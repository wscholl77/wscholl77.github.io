BPS El Paso

Scripture Focused Mission Trips

Assembling God's Word and then distributing in Mexico and to the Navajo Nation. Ministering to one of the fastest growing cities in the world. Ministering to the largest Indian Reservation in the world with a Navajo population of over 500,000. Working with local Independent Baptist churches throughout Juarez and the Navajo Nation. Witnessing people accept the Word of God.
