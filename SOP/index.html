Dock Safety SOP

1. Take the gladhand lock from the lockout board for the dock you're loading or unloading. Indicator should be red.

2. Go to the rear of the truck cab where the air brake gladhands are located.

3. Remove the red gladhand.

4. Attach a lock to the truckâ€™s red gladhand. This disables the brakes on the trailer hindering it from moving.

5. Place the key on the lockout board, slide the indicator to green, remove the safety chain and engage the dock plate. Proceed to load or unload the truck.

6. When finished loading or unloading: disengage dock plate, hook safety chain, slide indicator to red, grab the key from the lockout board.

7. Remove lock from gladhand and replace red air hose.

8. Give the driver BOL and notify the truck is finished. Place gladhand lock and key on lockout board. Indicator should be red.
